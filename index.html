The World of Zero

Discover the truth behind the Creation Engine and its purpose

The Creation Engine

Zero is not a natural world but a Creation Engine left by a precursor civilization. Its core system governs all reality through universal holographic-like interfaces.

System Mechanics

All biological entities have Levels, Stats, and Skills. Experience is gained through consequential actions, with diminishing returns for repetition.

Gacha System

Every 5 levels, entities can draw 5 skills from a gacha system ranging from 1-star Common skills to 9-star Mythic fragments of divine authority.

The Protagonist - Alex

Alex possesses the unique 10-star skill "Unlock" and is accompanied by a 0-Level Aid glitch that can hack the System and cause local instability.

Core System Features

Absolute Rules

  • Quantification of all biological entities
  • Experience through consequential actions
  • Inviolable menus (Class Evolution, Gacha Draws, etc.)
  • System self-correction of minor anomalies

Core Stats

  • Strength, Agility, Constitution
  • Intelligence, Wisdom, Perception
  • Derived: Health, Mana, Stamina
  • Physical & Magical Defense

Skill Rarity

  • 1-3 Stars: Common, Mundane skills
  • 4-6 Stars: Rare, Powerful/Esoteric
  • 7-8 Stars: Legendary, Reality-bending
  • 9 Stars: Mythic, fragments of divine authority
